SuperEstimation Serves Every Major Construction Sector

Industry-specific expertise for accurate electrical estimates across all project types

SuperEstimation has delivered accurate electrical estimates for projects in virtually every construction sector in the United States. Our industry-specific expertise ensures that your estimate reflects the true cost conditions, code requirements, and system complexities unique to your project type.

Healthcare and Medical Facilities

Healthcare electrical systems are among the most complex and code-intensive in the construction industry. SuperEstimation's healthcare estimating team understands the unique requirements of essential electrical systems, patient care environments, and life safety compliance under NFPA 99 and NEC Article 517.

Healthcare Projects SuperEstimation Estimates:

  • Hospitals
  • Outpatient surgery centers
  • Medical office buildings
  • Dental clinics
  • Urgent care facilities
  • Rehabilitation centers
  • Behavioral health facilities
  • Nursing homes and assisted living
  • Dialysis centers
  • Imaging and radiology centers

Healthcare Scope Areas SuperEstimation Covers:

Type 1 and Type 2 essential electrical systems, critical branch and equipment branch wiring, isolated power systems for wet procedure locations, nurse call and patient monitoring systems, medical gas alarm systems, clean room electrical installations, OR and ICU electrical systems, patient room power and data systems, hospital-grade receptacles and ground fault protection.

Multi-Family Residential

Multi-family residential projects present unique estimating challenges including repetitive unit layouts, complex service entrance requirements, shared area systems, and EV charging infrastructure. SuperEstimation uses unit-price estimating techniques to deliver fast and accurate estimates for large apartment and condominium projects.

Multi-Family Projects SuperEstimation Estimates:

  • Garden-style apartments
  • Mid-rise condominiums
  • High-rise apartment towers
  • Student housing
  • Affordable and workforce housing
  • Assisted living and memory care
  • Senior independent living
  • Mixed-use residential and commercial

Data Centers

Data center electrical systems are mission-critical and highly specialized. SuperEstimation's data center estimating team understands Tier I through Tier IV design requirements, redundant power distribution architecture, and the specific equipment and installation methods used in critical facilities.

Data Center Scope Areas SuperEstimation Covers:

  • Utility service entrance and switchgear
  • Standby diesel generator systems
  • Uninterruptible power supply (UPS) systems
  • Power distribution units (PDU)
  • Critical bus systems and automatic transfer switches
  • Raised floor power distribution
  • Server rack power whips and connections
  • Structured cabling infrastructure
  • Critical facility grounding and bonding
  • Environmental monitoring and building controls
  • Fire suppression system electrical
