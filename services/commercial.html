Professional Commercial Electrical Estimating

Commercial electrical estimating is the core specialty of SuperEstimation. Our commercial estimating team has delivered accurate, competitive electrical estimates for virtually every type of commercial construction project in the United States. We understand the complexity of commercial power distribution, lighting design, life safety systems, and coordination with other trades.

From small tenant improvements to large multi-story office buildings, SuperEstimation delivers the detailed, accurate estimates you need to win profitable commercial work and manage costs effectively throughout construction.

What SuperEstimation Covers in Commercial Estimates

Power Distribution

  • Utility service entrance
  • Primary switchgear and transformers
  • Main switchboards
  • Distribution panels
  • Branch circuits
  • Metering equipment

Lighting Systems

  • Complete lighting layout
  • Fixture pricing and installation
  • Lighting control systems
  • Emergency and exit lighting
  • Daylight harvesting systems
  • Energy code compliance

Life Safety Systems

  • Fire alarm systems per NFPA 101
  • Fire alarm devices and panels
  • NAC circuits
  • Emergency lighting systems
  • Exit signs and egress lighting
  • Mass notification systems

Data & Communications

  • Data and telecommunications rough-in
  • Conduit systems for low voltage
  • Structured cabling infrastructure
  • Telecom room equipment
  • Fiber optic installations

Mechanical Connections

  • HVAC electrical connections
  • VFDs and motor controls
  • Mechanical equipment wiring
  • Disconnect switches
  • Control wiring

Backup Power & Controls

  • Generator systems
  • Automatic transfer switches
  • UPS systems
  • Critical power distribution
  • Building automation systems
  • Energy management systems
