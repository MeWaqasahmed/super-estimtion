Residential Electrical Estimating Services

Professional estimates for single-family homes, multi-family units, and residential renovations

Professional Residential Electrical Estimating

SuperEstimation's residential electrical estimating service covers every type of residential construction project in the United States. Our residential estimating team brings deep expertise in residential electrical systems, residential code compliance, and the specific cost conditions of residential electrical markets across all 50 states.

Whether you're bidding a small single-family home renovation or a large multi-family apartment complex, SuperEstimation delivers accurate, detailed estimates that help you win profitable work and manage costs effectively throughout construction.

What SuperEstimation Covers in Residential Estimates

Service Entrance & Panels

  • Service entrance sizing and installation
  • Meter bases and weatherheads
  • Underground service installations
  • Main electrical panel sizing and pricing
  • Subpanel installations

Branch Circuits & Wiring

  • Complete branch circuit layout
  • All wire types and sizes
  • Conduit and raceway systems
  • Junction boxes and enclosures
  • Kitchen and bathroom circuits
  • Dedicated appliance circuits

Lighting Systems

  • Interior lighting rough-in
  • Recessed lighting installations
  • Surface and pendant fixtures
  • Outdoor and landscape lighting
  • Lighting control systems
  • Dimmer and switch installations

Safety & Protection

  • GFCI protection per NEC requirements
  • AFCI protection for all required areas
  • Smoke and CO detector systems
  • Whole-house surge protection
  • Grounding and bonding systems

Modern Home Systems

  • EV charging station rough-in (Level 1 & 2)
  • Solar PV system interconnection
  • Low voltage structured wiring
  • CAT6 and coaxial installations
  • Smart home pre-wiring
  • Home automation systems

HVAC & Equipment

  • HVAC equipment connections
  • Disconnect switches
  • Mechanical equipment wiring
  • Water heater connections
  • Pool and spa electrical
