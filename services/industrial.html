Industrial Electrical Estimating Services

Specialized estimates for manufacturing plants, warehouses, and complex industrial facilities

Professional Industrial Electrical Estimating

Industrial electrical projects demand a level of specialized knowledge and experience that few estimating services possess. SuperEstimation's industrial estimating team combines deep technical expertise with practical field experience to deliver electrical estimates for even the most complex industrial facilities.

From manufacturing plants to complex process facilities, SuperEstimation delivers the specialized, accurate estimates you need to win profitable industrial work and manage costs effectively throughout construction.

What SuperEstimation Covers in Industrial Estimates

High-Voltage Systems

  • High-voltage distribution systems
  • 4kV, 13kV, and 35kV switchgear
  • Substation installations
  • Oil-filled transformers
  • Outdoor switchgear

Motor Control & VFDs

  • Motor control centers (MCC)
  • Starter selection and pricing
  • Variable frequency drives (VFD)
  • Soft starters
  • Process equipment connections

Hazardous Locations

  • NEC Article 500 compliance
  • Class I, II, and III installations
  • Explosion-proof equipment
  • Intrinsically safe systems
  • Purged and pressurized enclosures

Instrumentation & Control

  • Instrumentation wiring
  • Control wiring and interlock systems
  • Junction boxes and marshaling panels
  • Process control systems
  • SCADA integration

Cable Management

  • Cable tray systems (ladder, trough)
  • Solid bottom cable trays
  • Rigid steel conduit systems
  • IMC and fiberglass raceways
  • Underground duct banks

Specialized Systems

  • Grounding and lightning protection
  • Emergency and standby power
  • Cathodic protection systems
  • Heat tracing systems
  • Process discipline coordination
