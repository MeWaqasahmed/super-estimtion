Professional Solar & PV System Estimating

SuperEstimation's solar estimating service helps electrical contractors capture the rapidly growing renewable energy market with accurate, comprehensive estimates for solar photovoltaic and battery storage installations.

From residential rooftop systems to large commercial installations, SuperEstimation delivers the detailed, accurate solar estimates you need to win profitable renewable energy projects and manage costs effectively throughout installation.

What SuperEstimation Covers in Solar Estimates

Solar Panels & Inverters

  • Solar panel module quantity and pricing
  • Pricing by wattage and manufacturer
  • String inverters
  • Microinverters
  • Power optimizer systems

Electrical Equipment

  • Combiner boxes
  • DC disconnect equipment
  • AC disconnect switches
  • Production metering equipment
  • Net metering equipment

Mounting Systems

  • Rooftop mounting hardware
  • Ground mount systems
  • Carport mounting structures
  • Ballasted systems
  • Tracking systems

Wiring & Conduit

  • DC wiring from array to inverter
  • AC wiring to service panel
  • Conduit systems
  • Wire management
  • Grounding systems

Battery Storage

  • Tesla Powerwall systems
  • Enphase IQ Battery
  • Commercial battery systems
  • Battery interconnection
  • Backup power integration

Interconnection & Permits

  • Grid interconnection equipment
  • Utility upgrade requirements
  • Monitoring and communication
  • Permit and plan check fees
  • Federal ITC documentation support
