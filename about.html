About SuperEstimation — Our Story

Building the future of electrical estimating, one contractor at a time

SuperEstimation was founded by a team of veteran electrical estimators and construction professionals who recognized a critical problem in the US electrical contracting industry. Contractors were spending too much time preparing bids and not enough time running their businesses.

The founders of SuperEstimation — all with backgrounds in journeyman and master electrician licensing, electrical project management, and professional estimating — built SuperEstimation to give electrical contractors of all sizes access to professional-grade estimating services without the overhead of hiring full-time in-house staff.

Since our founding, SuperEstimation has grown into one of the most trusted electrical estimating companies in the United States, serving hundreds of electrical contractors from solo operators to large multi-state firms. Our commitment to accuracy, speed, and professional integrity has made SuperEstimation the first call for contractors who want to win more work and grow their businesses.

SuperEstimation Mission Statement

SuperEstimation's mission is to help every electrical contractor in America win more bids, protect their profit margins, and build a thriving business through accurate, reliable, and fast professional estimating services. We believe that precise, professionally prepared electrical estimates are the foundation of every successful electrical contracting business, and SuperEstimation exists to make that foundation accessible to every contractor regardless of size.

SuperEstimation Vision

SuperEstimation's vision is to be the national standard in electrical estimating services — the company that every US electrical contractor thinks of first when they need an estimate they can trust. We are building toward a future where no electrical contractor has to lose a profitable project because of an inaccurate bid or miss a bid deadline because they ran out of time to estimate.

The SuperEstimation Team

Lead Estimators

SuperEstimation lead estimators hold certifications from the American Society of Professional Estimators (ASPE) and AACE International. Every lead estimator has a minimum of 10 years of hands-on electrical construction and estimating experience.

Quality Assurance

Every SuperEstimation estimate is reviewed by a senior QA estimator before delivery. Our quality assurance team verifies every line item for accuracy and ensures that the final deliverable meets SuperEstimation's strict quality standards.

Client Success Team

SuperEstimation's client success managers are available Monday through Saturday from 7 AM to 9 PM Eastern Time. They manage file intake, answer questions, and ensure that every SuperEstimation client experience exceeds expectations.
