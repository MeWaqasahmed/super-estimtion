What to Send SuperEstimation

To receive your SuperEstimation quote, please have the following ready:

  • Project name and address
  • Project type and use
  • Approximate square footage
  • Number of drawing sheets
  • Bid due date
  • PDF copies of electrical drawings and specifications

SuperEstimation accepts all standard file formats and file sizes up to 500MB through our secure portal.

Frequently Asked Questions

How fast does SuperEstimation deliver estimates?

SuperEstimation delivers most residential and small commercial estimates within 24 to 48 hours. Large commercial and industrial projects are delivered within 48 to 96 hours. Rush service with same-day delivery is available for an additional fee.

What format does SuperEstimation use for deliverables?

SuperEstimation delivers estimates in Excel and PDF as standard. We also provide estimates in Accubid, Trimble, and other software formats upon request at no additional charge.

Does SuperEstimation sign NDAs?

Yes. SuperEstimation executes mutual NDAs upon request before reviewing any project documents. All SuperEstimation projects are treated with complete confidentiality regardless of NDA status.

Does SuperEstimation serve all 50 states?

Yes. SuperEstimation serves electrical contractors in every US state and applies local labor rates, regional material pricing, and jurisdiction-specific code requirements to every project.
