Service 1: Residential Electrical Estimating

SuperEstimation's residential electrical estimating service covers every type of residential construction project in the United States. Our residential estimating team brings deep expertise in residential electrical systems, residential code compliance, and the specific cost conditions of residential electrical markets across all 50 states.

What SuperEstimation Covers in Residential Estimates:

  • Service entrance sizing and installation including meter bases, weatherheads, and underground service
  • Main electrical panel and subpanel sizing, pricing, and installation
  • Branch circuit layout and wiring including all wire types, conduit, and boxes
  • Kitchen and bathroom circuits including dedicated appliance circuits and GFCI protection
  • HVAC and mechanical equipment connections and disconnects
  • Lighting fixture rough-in and installation including recessed, surface, and pendant fixtures
  • Outdoor and landscape lighting systems
  • Smoke and carbon monoxide detector systems
  • Low voltage systems including structured wiring, CAT6, and coaxial
  • EV charging station rough-in and installation (Level 1 and Level 2)
  • Solar PV system rough-in and interconnection
  • Whole-house surge protection
  • AFCI and GFCI protection per NEC 2023 requirements
  • Permit and inspection fee allowances by jurisdiction

Residential Project Types SuperEstimation Serves:

Single-family homes (new construction and renovation), townhomes and duplexes, multi-family apartments and condominiums, custom luxury homes, residential additions and remodels, accessory dwelling units (ADU), manufactured and modular homes.

SuperEstimation Residential Deliverables:

Itemized material list by room and system, conduit and wire schedule, panel schedule pricing, labor hour breakdown by trade category, total cost summary with overhead and profit options, Excel and PDF format, CSI Division 26 format available upon request.

Service 2: Commercial Electrical Estimating

Commercial electrical estimating is the core specialty of SuperEstimation. Our commercial estimating team has delivered accurate, competitive electrical estimates for virtually every type of commercial construction project in the United States. We understand the complexity of commercial power distribution, lighting design, life safety systems, and coordination with other trades.

What SuperEstimation Covers in Commercial Estimates:

  • Utility service entrance including primary switchgear, transformers, and metering
  • Power distribution including main switchboards, distribution panels, and branch circuits
  • Lighting layout, fixture pricing, and lighting control systems
  • Emergency and exit lighting systems per NFPA 101
  • Fire alarm systems including devices, panels, and NAC circuits
  • Data and telecommunications rough-in and conduit systems
  • Mechanical and HVAC electrical connections including VFDs and motor controls
  • Generator systems including fuel storage and automatic transfer switches
  • UPS systems and critical power distribution
  • Building automation and energy management systems
  • Security and access control system rough-in
  • Audio visual and presentation system rough-in
  • Energy code compliance including Title 24 (California) and ASHRAE 90.1
  • Testing, commissioning, and startup allowances
  • Permit and inspection fees

Commercial Project Types SuperEstimation Serves:

Office buildings, retail stores and shopping centers, hotels and hospitality, medical office buildings and clinics, K-12 schools and universities, government and municipal buildings, restaurants and food service, banks and financial institutions, churches and religious facilities, auto dealerships, gas stations, gyms and fitness centers, theaters and entertainment venues.

Service 3: Industrial Electrical Estimating

Industrial electrical projects demand a level of specialized knowledge and experience that few estimating services possess. SuperEstimation's industrial estimating team combines deep technical expertise with practical field experience to deliver electrical estimates for even the most complex industrial facilities.

What SuperEstimation Covers in Industrial Estimates:

  • High-voltage distribution systems including 4kV, 13kV, and 35kV switchgear
  • Motor control centers (MCC) including starter selection and pricing
  • Variable frequency drives (VFD) and soft starters
  • Process equipment connections and interlock wiring
  • Hazardous location installations per NEC Article 500 (Class I, II, and III)
  • Instrumentation and control wiring including junction boxes and marshaling panels
  • Cable tray systems including ladder, ventilated trough, and solid bottom
  • Conduit and raceway systems including rigid steel, IMC, and fiberglass
  • Grounding and lightning protection systems
  • Substation installations including oil-filled transformers and outdoor switchgear
  • Emergency and standby power systems
  • Cathodic protection systems
  • Heat tracing systems
  • Coordination with mechanical, civil, structural, and process disciplines

Industrial Project Types SuperEstimation Serves:

Manufacturing plants, distribution warehouses, food and beverage processing, chemical and pharmaceutical facilities, oil and gas installations, water and wastewater treatment plants, mining and minerals processing, cold storage facilities, automotive assembly, aerospace manufacturing.

Service 4: Solar and PV System Estimating

SuperEstimation's solar estimating service helps electrical contractors capture the rapidly growing renewable energy market with accurate, comprehensive estimates for solar photovoltaic and battery storage installations.

What SuperEstimation Covers in Solar Estimates:

  • Solar panel module quantity and pricing by wattage and manufacturer
  • String inverters, microinverters, and power optimizer systems
  • Combiner boxes and DC disconnect equipment
  • Mounting system hardware including rooftop, ground mount, and carport systems
  • DC wiring and conduit from array to inverter
  • AC wiring and conduit from inverter to service panel
  • Net metering and production metering equipment
  • Battery storage systems including Tesla Powerwall, Enphase IQ Battery, and commercial battery systems
  • Grid interconnection equipment and utility upgrade requirements
  • Monitoring and communication equipment
  • Permit, plan check, and utility interconnection fees
  • Federal ITC and state incentive documentation support

Service 7: Design-Build Estimating

Design-build delivery is increasingly popular across all project types in the US construction market. SuperEstimation supports design-build teams with electrical estimating at every phase of design development, from early conceptual budgets through construction document estimates and GMP development.

Design Phases SuperEstimation Supports: Conceptual or ROM estimates, Schematic design estimates at 15% to 30% design completion, Design development estimates at 60% completion, Construction document estimates at 100% completion, GMP development and validation.

Service 8: Value Engineering Support

When project budgets are over target, value engineering is the path forward. SuperEstimation's value engineering service analyzes your electrical design and identifies cost-saving alternatives that maintain system quality and functionality while reducing total installed cost.

SuperEstimation Value Engineering Services: Alternative material and equipment analysis with installed cost comparisons, System redesign options with estimated savings and trade-offs, Life cycle cost analysis comparing first cost versus long-term operating costs, Energy efficiency upgrade pricing and payback analysis, Budget reconciliation support and owner presentations.
