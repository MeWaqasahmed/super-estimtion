Healthcare and Medical Facilities

Specialized Electrical Estimating for Healthcare Construction

Healthcare electrical systems are the most complex, most code-intensive, and most expensive electrical systems in the entire construction industry. SuperEstimation's healthcare estimating team has the specialized expertise to ensure every required system is included in your estimate.

Why Healthcare Electrical Estimating Requires Specialized Expertise

The consequences of an electrical failure in a hospital operating room, an ICU, or a dialysis center can be life-threatening. As a result, healthcare electrical installations are governed by the strictest code requirements in the NEC, including NEC Article 517 for healthcare facilities, and by NFPA 99 Health Care Facilities Code.

Estimators who do not have deep healthcare experience routinely miss critical scope items in healthcare electrical estimates, including isolated power systems, essential electrical system wiring requirements, and the specific device and wiring specifications required in patient care areas.

SuperEstimation's healthcare estimating team has the experience and knowledge to ensure that every required system is included in your estimate before you submit your bid.

Key Cost Factors in Healthcare Electrical Estimating

Healthcare electrical projects are significantly more expensive per square foot than any other building type. Key cost drivers include:

Mandatory Redundancy

The essential electrical system requires complete redundancy of critical power distribution

Hospital-Grade Specifications

Device and wiring specifications required in patient care areas exceed standard commercial grade

Isolated Power Requirements

Wet procedure locations require expensive isolated power system installations

Specialty Systems

Extensive low-voltage and specialty systems unique to healthcare environments

Equipment Coordination

Complex coordination requirements with medical equipment vendors

Occupied Environments

Higher labor productivity factor for working in occupied, infection-controlled healthcare settings

SuperEstimation's healthcare estimating team accounts for all of these factors in every healthcare project estimate, ensuring that your bid reflects the true cost of the work and that your project is financially viable from day one.
