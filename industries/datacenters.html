Data Centers and Critical Facilities

Expert Electrical Estimating for Mission-Critical Infrastructure

Data center electrical systems are the most demanding, most redundant, and most expensive electrical installations in the commercial construction industry. SuperEstimation's data center estimating team has the specialized expertise to accurately estimate these complex, mission-critical facilities.

Why Data Center Electrical Estimating Requires Specialized Expertise

Data center electrical systems must deliver 99.99% or higher uptime, which requires complete redundancy of all critical power systems. The electrical infrastructure represents the largest single cost component of data center construction, often exceeding 40% of total project cost.

Data center electrical design follows industry standards including TIA-942 for data center infrastructure, Uptime Institute tier classifications, and specific requirements from hyperscale operators. Power density in modern data centers can exceed 300 watts per square foot, requiring sophisticated power distribution and cooling systems.

SuperEstimation's data center estimating team has extensive experience with colocation facilities, enterprise data centers, hyperscale facilities, and edge computing sites. We understand the unique requirements of mission-critical electrical infrastructure.

Key Cost Factors in Data Center Electrical Estimating

Data center electrical costs are driven by several critical factors:

Redundancy Level

Tier III and Tier IV facilities require complete redundancy of all electrical systems

Power Density

Higher power density requires more sophisticated distribution and larger infrastructure

UPS Architecture

Centralized vs. distributed UPS systems have significantly different cost profiles

Generator Capacity

Generator systems and fuel storage represent major cost components

Monitoring and Controls

Sophisticated monitoring systems are required for all critical electrical infrastructure

Commissioning Requirements

Extensive testing and commissioning is required for all data center electrical systems
