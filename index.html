Who Is SuperEstimation

SuperEstimation is a leading electrical estimating company serving electrical contractors, general contractors, subcontractors, and construction firms across the United States. Whether you are bidding a small residential renovation in Texas or a multi-million dollar industrial facility in California, SuperEstimation delivers the accurate, detailed, and professionally formatted electrical estimates you need to win more bids and protect your profit margins.

At SuperEstimation, we combine industry-certified estimators, cutting-edge estimating software, and a deep understanding of the US electrical construction market to deliver estimates that are built to win. Our team uses Accubid, Trimble, Bluebeam, PlanSwift, and Microsoft Excel to produce line-by-line material takeoffs, labor hour breakdowns, and complete bid packages that give you a competitive edge on every project you bid.
