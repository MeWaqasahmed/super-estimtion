Frequently Asked Questions About SuperEstimation

Everything you need to know about our electrical estimating services

What is SuperEstimation?

SuperEstimation is a professional electrical estimating company serving electrical contractors, general contractors, and subcontractors across all 50 US states. SuperEstimation provides accurate, fast, and affordable electrical estimating services for residential, commercial, industrial, solar, and all other electrical project types.

How fast does SuperEstimation deliver estimates?

SuperEstimation delivers most residential and small commercial estimates within 24 to 48 hours. Medium to large commercial and industrial projects are delivered within 48 to 96 hours. Rush same-day and next-morning service is available for urgent deadlines.

How much does SuperEstimation charge for estimates?

SuperEstimation's pricing starts at $150 for small residential projects and $350 for commercial projects. Large and complex projects are priced based on scope. Monthly retainer plans starting at $1,200 per month are available for contractors who submit 4 or more bids monthly. Visit our pricing page for complete details.

What states does SuperEstimation serve?

SuperEstimation serves electrical contractors in all 50 US states. We apply local labor rates, regional material pricing, and jurisdiction-specific code requirements to every project. Our team has deep knowledge of regional construction markets across the entire United States.

What file formats does SuperEstimation accept?

SuperEstimation accepts PDFs of electrical and architectural drawings, project specifications, addenda, and all other relevant project documents. We also accept CAD and Bluebeam files. File sizes up to 500MB can be uploaded through our secure client portal.

What deliverables does SuperEstimation provide?

SuperEstimation delivers complete material takeoffs, labor hour breakdowns, cost summaries, and bid summary sheets in Excel and PDF formats. CSI Division 26 format, Accubid format, and custom formats are available upon request at no additional charge.

Does SuperEstimation sign NDAs?

Yes. SuperEstimation executes mutual NDAs upon request before reviewing any project documents. All SuperEstimation projects are treated with complete confidentiality regardless of NDA status. We use encrypted file transfer systems to protect your project plans and business information.

Does SuperEstimation offer free revisions?

Yes. Every SuperEstimation project includes one free revision delivered within 48 hours. If you need adjustments to scope, pricing assumptions, or format, our team responds quickly to incorporate your changes.

Does SuperEstimation handle change order estimates?

Yes. SuperEstimation provides fast change order estimating to support project teams throughout the construction process. We deliver detailed change order estimates that clearly document your costs and support successful negotiation with owners and general contractors.

How do I get started with SuperEstimation?

Contact SuperEstimation by phone at 1-800-SUPER-EST, email at quotes@superestimation.com, or through our online quote form. We respond within 2 business hours and can have your estimate started the same day.

What certifications do SuperEstimation estimators hold?

SuperEstimation estimators hold certifications from the American Society of Professional Estimators (ASPE) and AACE International. All SuperEstimation estimators have a minimum of 7 years of hands-on electrical construction experience. Our team combines professional certification with real-world field knowledge.

Does SuperEstimation work with small electrical contractors?

Absolutely. SuperEstimation serves contractors of every size, from independent electricians to large multi-state firms. Our pricing is designed to be accessible and cost-effective for small contractors who need professional estimating services without the overhead of full-time staff.

What software does SuperEstimation use?

SuperEstimation estimators are proficient in all major electrical estimating and takeoff platforms including Accubid Enterprise, Trimble Electrical Estimating, ConEst IntelliBid, PlanSwift, Bluebeam Revu, On-Screen Takeoff, and Microsoft Excel with custom SuperEstimation templates.

Can SuperEstimation help with value engineering?

Yes. SuperEstimation's value engineering service analyzes your electrical design and identifies cost-saving alternatives that maintain system quality and functionality while reducing total installed cost. We provide alternative material and equipment analysis, system redesign options, and life cycle cost comparisons.

Does SuperEstimation understand prevailing wage requirements?

Yes. SuperEstimation is experienced with Davis-Bacon wage rates and the documentation requirements of publicly funded construction projects. We apply the correct prevailing wage rates for your project location and type, and include all required documentation in your estimate.

What project types does SuperEstimation estimate?

SuperEstimation estimates all electrical project types including residential (single-family, multi-family, renovations), commercial (office, retail, hospitality, healthcare, schools), industrial (manufacturing, warehouses, processing facilities), solar and renewable energy, data centers, and government projects. Visit our services page for complete details.

How accurate are SuperEstimation's estimates?

SuperEstimation's estimates are built using current material pricing, local labor rates, and NECA manual of labor units adjusted for project-specific conditions. Every estimate goes through a two-step internal quality review before delivery. Our clients consistently report that SuperEstimation estimates align closely with their actual field costs.

Can I use SuperEstimation for just one project?

Yes. SuperEstimation works on a project-by-project basis with no long-term contracts required. Many contractors start with a single project to evaluate our service quality before establishing an ongoing relationship. Monthly retainer plans are available for contractors who prefer dedicated estimating support.

Does SuperEstimation provide bid preparation services?

Yes. SuperEstimation's bid preparation service goes beyond the numbers to deliver a complete, professionally formatted bid package including proposal letters, scope of work documents, exclusions lists, and addendum reviews — everything you need to submit a professional, competitive bid.

What makes SuperEstimation different from other estimating services?

SuperEstimation combines ASPE and AACE certified estimators with a minimum of 7 years of field experience, guaranteed 24 to 72 hour turnaround, transparent project-based pricing, two-step quality review on every estimate, and complete confidentiality. We serve all 50 US states with local market knowledge and jurisdiction-specific code compliance.
